ABOUT CODINGAL


Codingal is an online platform that offers coding classes for kids and teenagers. It provides live, 1-on-1 or small group classes led by instructors with computer science backgrounds. Codingal's curriculum focuses on project-based learning, where students build apps, games, websites, and even robotics to learn coding concepts. The platform aims to inspire a love of coding in young learners and equip them with the skills needed for future careers in technology.
